About Me

Payton Oxley is a student at Seminole State College on the road to becoming a Video Game Designer with a degree in Digital Media. Her love for video games grew up with her and never left, leaving her with a passion in what she hopes to be in creating video games that change people's lives. As someone who is always willing to learn new things, she is always excited to see what the technological world has to offer her, even if some of those things may prove challenging. Her ultimate goal is to one day make a lifelong career out of her love for video games and create new projects where she can help as many people as she can, wherever she can!
